Weekly Waste Sorting Tips

Plastic

Rinse and dry plastic bottles and containers. Look for recycling symbols. Consider reducing your plastic consumption.

Organic Waste

Explore composting options for food scraps and yard waste. Check local guidelines for organic waste collection.

E-waste

Do not dispose of electronics with regular waste. Find designated e-waste collection points in Mombasa.

Paper & Cardboard

Keep paper and cardboard clean and dry for recycling. Flatten cardboard boxes to save space.
